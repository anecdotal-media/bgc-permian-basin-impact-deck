[03] The Need

West Texas Kids Need More.

After-school hours are when kids are most at risk. Working parents in Odessa and Midland need a safe, affordable place where their children can learn, eat a healthy meal, and grow.

A Daily Safe Place.

Our Clubs open every weekday — with mentors, homework help, hot meals, the arts, sports, and a community that believes in every kid who walks through the door.
